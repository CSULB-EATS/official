What is
EATS?

EATS is a cross-disciplinary, project-oriented club that bridges the gap between computer, electrical, and software engineering at California State University, Long Beach.

We believe that real learning happens when you build things. Our community of engineers and developers work together on embedded systems projects that go beyond the classroom.
